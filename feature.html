1: If the card is lost, how to block the account during office hours? The account holder goes to the nearest bank branch with his national card and requests to block his card. Customers can also block their account through new services (internet banking, phone banking, and Mobile Bank Tejarat) if they have passwords for electronic services. 2: Is it possible to issue cards for passbook accounts? Yes. Currently, customers can apply for a card by visiting the account issuing branch. 3: Is it possible to reissue the card through all branches? Yes. Customers can go to any bank branch to get a new card with their national card. 4: How is it possible to get the card security code (CVV2)? The CVV2 code is inserted on the business card. Otherwise, customers should go to one of Tejarat Bank's ATMs and select from the menu of other services, the option of setting a second fixed Internet password, define a password between 5 and 12 digits, and then enter the CVV2 code and the card's expiration date on the printed receipt. Customers are provided. 5: How is it possible to get the second internet password of the card? By going to one of the bank's ATMs, by selecting the menu of other services and the option of setting a second internet password, customers can define a password between 5 and 12 digits. 6: What is the necessary action to remove the blocking of cards that have been blocked due to incorrect registration of passwords through ATMs or store card readers? In such a situation, in order to unblock the card, customers should go to one of the bank branches to reactivate the card while receiving a new password. 7: What is the necessary action to unblock the cards blocked through 1554? In such a situation, in order to unblock the card, customers should go to one of the bank branches to reactivate the card while receiving a new password. 8: Is it possible to grant a card to which customers? It is only possible to grant a card to the accounts of people over 7 years old. 9: What payment instrument is possible for free accounts and non-receipt persons above 18 years of age? The bank is only allowed to grant physical payment cards to these people.
